Topsi Banana

Description

Green bananas cooked in a traditional roasted peanut sauce with palm oil, fresh spices, smoked fish and crayfish.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 hand unripe banana
  • 2 tablespoons ginger and garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 bouillon cubes
  • 1 cup groundnut paste
  • I tablespoon Celery paste
  • 1 hebanero chopped pepper
  • 4 diced tomato
  • 4 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Crayfish

Steps

  1. Wash unripe bananas, peel them and cut them up. Then rinse thoroughly and set aside
  2. Marinate and Par-boil your chicken. Then keep aside the stock.
  3. Shallow fry your chicken.
  4. Pour vegetable oil in a pot and heat slightly. Then fry your onion, then add tomato.
  5. Add groundnut paste abd stir thoroughly
  6. Now place plantains inside and allow to simmer.
  7. Add fried chicken.
  8. Add salt, cubes and crayfish. Allow to simmer for 20 mins
  9. Stir in every 5 mins interval
  10. Remove from heat. Let it cold for 5 mins.
